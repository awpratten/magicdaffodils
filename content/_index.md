Magic Daffodils is an experimental music band based in London, Ontario. The group is known for its unique sound that blends elements of free ambient noise, improvisation, and various musical genres.

The band focuses on free form improvisation, creating a sound that is often described as ambient and noise music. Its performances are characterized by spontaneous musical exploration, allowing for a dynamic and engaging experience.

Members are
* Art Pratten - Pratt-a-various
* Alex Schmoll - synths and effects 
* Bernie Koenig - drums and vibes
* Cailen Dye - bass and effects
* Richard Moule - synthesizers, samplers, guitar pedals and effects
* Warren Pratten - guitar and effects
